ScreenCalm

ScreenCalm is a free Chrome extension that adds a customizable overlay to reduce visual discomfort caused by motion, brightness, and contrast. Ideal for people with screen intolerance, post-concussion symptoms, or sensitivity to digital screens.

Adjust overlay height, opacity, blur, and color to make screen time more comfortable.

Get ScreenCalm
Bright contrasts and motion can cause discomfort in people that suffer from screen intolerance.
ScreenCalm adds an adjustable overlay that allows the user to narrow focus and filter out discomfort causing visuals.
Adjust color, height, opacity, edge, and background blur.
